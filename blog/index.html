  • Virtuoso Strings Dataset

    I lead the curation of the Virtuoso Strings, a dataset for timing analysis and automatic music transcription (AMT) tasks requiring note onset annotations. This dataset takes advantage of real-world recordings in multitrack format and is curated as a component of the Augmented Reality Music Ensemble (ARME) project. For ISMIR late-breaking demo, please see ismir2023program.ismir.net/lbd_312.html
